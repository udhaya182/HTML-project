About the course

  • Offered in semester 2 only.
  • CHF03 is a blended course.
  • This course introduces the fundamental principles of general chemistry and shows how they are applied to a variety of themes and topics.
  • The unit subsequently develops the major concepts in physical, inorganic and organic chemistry that are required for studying higher level chemistry.
  • The major focus of this course is thermochemistry and chemical equilibrium, acids and bases and organic chemistry.
  • The course is also laboratory oriented and is tailored to enhance students' basic experimental skills.
