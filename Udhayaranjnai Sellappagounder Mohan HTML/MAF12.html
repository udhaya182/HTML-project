About the course

  • Offered in semester 2 only.
  • MAF12 a blended course.
  • This course is required for students who want to study all sciences, engineering or economics.
  • It focuses of the concepts of Differentiation, Integration and Statistics.
  • MAF12 and MAF11 prepare students for study of mathematics at degree 1 level.
